About Us

Welcome to Uromi JDPCI

Uromi Justice Peace and Caritas Initiatives (Uromi JDPCI) extends a gracious welcome to you. As a faith-based Non-Governmental Organization affiliated with the esteemed Catholic Diocese of Uromi, our solemn mandate is to ardently promote Justice and Peace, encourage meaningful development, and steadfastly provide support for the less privileged across the distinguished 5 Local Government Areas of Edo Central Senatorial district.

In the noble pursuit of our mission, Uromi JDPCI is resolute in its commitment to making a substantial impact within our community. We invite you to join us in this noble endeavor, contributing to creating a more just and harmonious society.

Programs

Our Projects

Good Governance

Governance seeks to enhance lives through democracy, but voters are unaware of party ideologies. Illiteracy and vote buying persist despite reforms. CSOs like Uromi JDPCI promote accountability, while parties must educate voters.

Health

Edo Central faces health challenges, including inadequate facilities, stigma around TB and HIV, and rising mental health issues due to poverty. Uromi JDPCI emphasizes the need for continued health education and public awareness to improve access to care.

Sexual and Gender-Based Violence, and Child Early and Forced Marriage (SGBV/CEFM)

Poverty in Edo State has increased sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV) and child early and forced marriages (CEFM). Uromi JDPCI trains communities to address these issues, emphasizing the need for stronger partnerships and awareness.

Legal & Correctional Service Apostolate

The Police in Nigeria conduct indiscriminate arrests, leading to overcrowded custodial facilities that violate SDGs. Pregnant inmates lack medical care, and awaiting trial inmates miss rehabilitation. Uromi JDPCI offers relief and legal aid, reducing overcrowding and supporting skill acquisition.

Micro-Credit

Access to business capital is a challenge for the active poor. Uromi JDPCI's micro-credit scheme offers small loans to help sustain businesses. More funding is needed to expand reach and provide financial literacy education.

Livelihood Support

A World Bank report (March 2022) shows that Nigeria's poverty reduction is hindered by slow growth and low human capital, with 4 in 10 Nigerians below the poverty line. High unemployment leads to rising crime.

Human Rights

Many people are unaware of their human rights and legal protections. Law enforcement often makes indiscriminate arrests for alleged cybercrime, and seeking justice is costly and delayed. There is a need for better education and support for the vulnerable to access justice.

Migration and Countering Trafficking in Persons

Irregular migration and human trafficking of youths in Edo State stem from poverty and unemployment. Traffickers exploit vulnerabilities with fake documents and social media. Uromi JDPCI promotes awareness and skill acquisition, urging agencies to offer economic alternatives.

Environment and Climate Change

Climate change is causing property destruction and loss of lives, worsened by deforestation and waste issues. Increased awareness and promotion of renewable energy are needed, along with better collaboration among stakeholders.
