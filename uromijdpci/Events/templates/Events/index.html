About Us

Welcome to Uromi JDPCI

Uromi Justice Development Peace And Caritas Initiatives (Uromi JDPCI) extends a gracious welcome to you. As a faith-based Non-Governmental Organization affiliated with the esteemed Catholic Diocese of Uromi, our solemn mandate is to ardently promote Justice and Peace, encourage meaningful development, and steadfastly provide support for the less privileged across the distinguished 5 Local Government Areas of Edo Central Senatorial district.

In the noble pursuit of our mission, Uromi JDPCI is resolute in its commitment to making a substantial impact within our community. We invite you to join us in this noble endeavor, contributing to creating a more just and harmonious society.

Brief Overview of Our Work

Uromi Justice Development Peace And Caritas Initiatives (Uromi JDPCI) is a faith-based NGO committed to promoting justice, peace, and meaningful development. Our interventions span across Edo Central Senatorial District, addressing key issues such as health, education, human rights, and economic empowerment.

Health Education
Programs to improve awareness on HIV/AIDS, TB, and general health care.
Human Rights
Empowering communities to stand against human rights violations.
Economic Empowerment
Micro-credit schemes to support local farmers and traders.
Education Initiatives
Programs for skill acquisition and school awareness.
